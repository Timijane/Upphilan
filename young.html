VISION FOR THE YOUNG

Vision for the Young is a purpose-driven platform founded in 2019 by Okewunmi Pelumi, popularly known as P. Paul the Great (P.PAULTHEGREAT). The vision was born out of a deep concern for the future of young people and a passion to inspire them to see beyond their immediate limitations.

Since its inception, Vision for the Young has reached secondary schools and universities across Ota, Ogun State, Nigeria, offering hope, direction, and practical motivation to students at critical stages of their lives.

Through school visits, talks, and personal engagements, P. Paul challenges young people to dream more, think more, act more, and go further than society often expects of them. Importantly, he does not only speak—he lives the message he teaches.

His personal journey stands as a powerful testimony. Admitted to Ladoke Akintola University of Technology to study microbiology, he later realized the path did not align with his purpose. Despite financial hardship and societal pressure, he made the bold decision to start again—returning home, studying arts subjects, attending tutorials, and retaking O'Level and JAMB examinations. Today, he is a law student at a private university in Nigeria.

Vision for the Young has since helped many Nigerian youths break mental barriers, rediscover purpose, and believe that transformation is possible through courage, discipline, and faith in one's vision.

Regardless of age, color, or background—if you can dare to dream, you can become. We are the architects of the future.
