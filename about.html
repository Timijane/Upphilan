The Journey

From passion to purpose, shaping lives through creativity and advocacy

Paul Okewunmi, affectionately known as P. Paul (The Great), is a poet, spoken word artist, and public speaker recognized for his powerful voice and inspiring presence. He is deeply passionate about youth development and human transformation, using his words, creativity, and platform to motivate individuals—especially young people—to discover purpose, build confidence, and pursue meaningful impact in their lives and communities.

Founder & Visionary

I am the Founder of VISION FOR THE YOUNG, a purpose-driven initiative established to build confident, value-conscious, and impact-oriented young people. Through this platform, I help young individuals develop clarity of purpose, self-belief, and the courage to contribute meaningfully to society.

Building the next generation of leaders

The Legal Mind

As an undergraduate of Law, I bring structure, advocacy, and ethical awareness into my work—bridging creativity with responsibility, and inspiration with action.

Core Beliefs

Unlimited Potential

Young people can become whoever they dare to dream of—regardless of age, color, or background.

Transformative Power of Words

Words have the power to heal, build, and transform lives when used responsibly.

Creativity as Service

Creativity is not just expression—it is responsibility toward healing and unity.

The Mission

Through poetry, spoken word performances, public speaking engagements, and community initiatives, I contribute to lives and destinies. My work is grounded in lived experiences, measurable impact, and verifiable testimonies of transformation.

I speak the language of peace, healing, and human transformation, using creativity as a tool to restore hope, ignite purpose, and inspire a generation to rise beyond limitations.

What I Stand For

The pillars that guide my work and define my impact

Purpose

Helping individuals discover meaning, direction, and clarity in life. Every life has a unique calling, and my mission is to help uncover it.

Change that lasts, impact that matters
