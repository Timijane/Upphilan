About P. Paul (The Great)

Poet. Spoken Word Artist. Public Speaker. Humanitarian Advocate. A voice committed to peace, healing, and human transformation.

My name is Paul Okewunmi, professionally known as P. Paul (The Great). I am a poet, spoken word artist, music composer, public speaker, and humanitarian advocate deeply committed to human development and youth empowerment.

I am the Founder of Young Generation of Exploits, a purpose-driven initiative established to build confident, value-conscious, and impact-oriented young people. Through this platform, I help young individuals develop clarity of purpose, self-belief, and the courage to contribute meaningfully to society.

As an undergraduate of Law, I bring structure, advocacy, and ethical awareness into my work—bridging creativity with responsibility, and inspiration with action.

At the core of everything I do is a defining belief: young people can become whoever they dare to dream of—regardless of age, color, or background.

Through poetry, spoken word performances, public speaking engagements, music, and community initiatives, I contribute to lives and destinies. My work is grounded in lived experiences, measurable impact, and verifiable testimonies of transformation.

I speak the language of peace, healing, and human transformation, using creativity as a tool to restore hope, ignite purpose, and inspire a generation to rise beyond limitations.
