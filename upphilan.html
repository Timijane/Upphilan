Global Ecosystem

Upphilan Global Network

Paul Okewunmi is the CEO and Founder of Upphilan Global Network, a business investment platform where partners collaborate to develop, refine, and invest in innovative ideas while strengthening and scaling existing ventures. Through Upphilan, he has built a dynamic ecosystem that connects visionaries, entrepreneurs, and investors committed to creating sustainable and impactful enterprises.

Driven by a passion for innovation and economic empowerment, Paul leads the network with a strong focus on strategic partnerships, capacity building, and long-term value creation. Upphilan serves as a hub for nurturing ideas, supporting early-stage businesses, and connecting individuals with the mentorship, resources, and networks needed to transform concepts into thriving ventures.

Under his leadership, the network continues to cultivate a culture of collaboration, forward-thinking, and purposeful enterprise, positioning itself as a catalyst for business growth and opportunity. Paul’s work through Upphilan reflects his broader commitment to youth development, entrepreneurship, and the transformation of potential into measurable impact within communities and beyond.
