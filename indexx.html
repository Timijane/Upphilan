About P. Paul (The Great)

Paul Okewunmi, popularly known as P. Paul (The Great), is a poet, spoken word artist, music composer, and public speaker committed to youth empowerment, humanitarian advocacy, and human transformation. Through creative expression and purposeful dialogue, he helps individuals discover their voice, worth, and potential—regardless of age, color, or background.

Poetry & Spoken Word

Powerful performances that inspire reflection, resilience, and identity exploration across audiences and communities.

Public Speaking

Engaging talks on leadership, self-belief, and youth empowerment that motivate action and positive change.

Music Composition

Creative soundscapes that complement spoken word and storytelling to enhance emotional impact.

Humanitarian Advocacy

Community and youth-focused initiatives designed to cultivate values, service, and positive societal contribution.
